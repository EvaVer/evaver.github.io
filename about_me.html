My Data Analysis Life

How I went from studying Nutrition and Health to becoming a data analyst

Hi there! I am a young data analyst with a passion for health and sustainability. I am familiar with SQL, python, Power BI, and more! Take a look at my recent data analysis projects to see an overview of my skills.

Where I started

I started my jouney as a data analyst during my master's degree in Nutrition and Health. During my statistics courses, I was introduced to SPSS and R, but it wasn't until my thesis and internship that i began to love crunching numbers.

During my thesis and internship I analysed data of patients enrolled in (intervention) studies of large hospitals, for which I used various statistical methods and clustering methods. This required precise work and careful consideration of multiple aspects that could have influenced the results. I loved doing this work and decided to develop myself into a data analyst after my studies.

Currently I am employed by Working Talent, a company that offers IT traineeships to young professionals who want to change careers or need a career boost. Under their tutelage, I have developed into a data analyst capable of working with a variety of tools. A job at a top-100 company in The Netherlands will help me develop my skills and gain work experience.

In my personal life

I love being creative, and spend a lot of my time crocheting and drawing. I am quickly enthoustiastic about a new project or learning a new skill, I love learning new things!

I also love to spend my time playing games with friends. I particularly enjoy games that require a lot of teamwork and/or strategy.
