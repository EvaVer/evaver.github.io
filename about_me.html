My Data Analysis Roadmap

How I went from studying Nutrition and Health to becoming a data analyst

Hi there! I am a young data analyst with a passion for health and sustainability. I am most experienced with SQL, python, Power BI, and more! Take a look at my recent data analysis projects to see an overview of my skills.

Besides SQL, python and Power BI, i have some experience using Tableau and more advanced features of Microsoft Excel, including pivot tables. In my spare time I have been studying JavaScript, HTML and CSS, trying to become more skilled at web development.

Where I started

I started my jouney as a data analyst during my bachelor's and master's degree in Nutrition and Health at Wageningen University. During my statistics courses, I was introduced to SPSS and R, but it wasn't until my thesis and internship that i began to love crunching numbers.

During my thesis and internship I analysed data of patients enrolled in (intervention) studies of large hospitals, for which I used various statistical methods and clustering methods. These analyses required precise work and careful consideration of multiple aspects that could have influenced the results. I loved doing this work and decided to further develop myself into a data analyst after my studies.

Currently I am employed by Working Talent, a company that offers IT traineeships to young professionals who want to change careers or need a career boost. Under their tutelage, I have developed into a data analyst capable of working with a variety of tools. A job at a top-100 company in The Netherlands will help me develop my skills and gain work experience.
